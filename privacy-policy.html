Privacy Policy for The Mechaverse

Last Updated: February 8, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how The Mechaverse ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you access or play our virtual reality game through Meta Horizon platforms and related services. This policy is designed to comply with Meta Horizons privacy requirements, including VRC.Privacy.1 through VRC.Privacy.4. By accessing or using The Mechaverse, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate, improve, and support The Mechaverse.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • In‑game display name or username
  • Player preferences and settings
  • Communications you send to us (such as support requests or feedback)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you play The Mechaverse, we may automatically receive:

  • Gameplay data (progress, achievements, session length, interactions)
  • Device and platform information (device type, operating system, app version)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (country or region level only)

1.3 Information from Meta Platforms

If you access The Mechaverse through Meta Horizon services, we may receive limited account-related information as permitted by Meta and your privacy settings. We do not receive your real name, email address, or payment details unless explicitly provided by Meta under its own policies.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain The Mechaverse
  • Save gameplay progress and settings
  • Improve gameplay balance, performance, and features
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address bugs, abuse, cheating, or security issues

We do not use personal data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or profiling outside the game experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure (VRC.Privacy.1 & VRC.Privacy.2)

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information only in the following cases:

  • With Meta Platforms: As required to operate on Meta Horizon services and comply with Meta policies
  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who help us operate the game (such as analytics or crash reporting), bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • Safety and Integrity: To protect the rights, safety, or security of players, Meta, or the public

All shared data is limited to what is necessary for the stated purpose.

4. Data Retention (VRC.Privacy.3)

We retain information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the game and its features
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Gameplay and usage data may be anonymized or aggregated for long‑term analysis. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or de‑identified.

5. Data Security (VRC.Privacy.4)

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure storage and access controls
  • Limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Using trusted third‑party services with appropriate security standards

No system is 100% secure, but we actively work to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Mechaverse is not intended for children under the minimum age permitted by Meta Horizon platform policies. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain data uses

Requests can be made using the contact information below. Meta account‑level data must be managed directly through Meta.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of The Mechaverse after changes means you accept the revised policy.
